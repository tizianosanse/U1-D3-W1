Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart
cranberries,and creamy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups(500g)all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarn water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes(scrape rind off a paring knife if desired,though it's not
    necessary )
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dough

in medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix together all
the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the
dough,through I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl whwn mixing by hand to incorporate
the last of the flour...
